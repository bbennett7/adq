Question

What does it actually mean to fine-tune a model versus just prompting it differently?

Answer

Fine-tuning modifies the model's weights — the actual numbers that define what the model knows and how it responds. Prompting works on top of a frozen model, steering its behavior at inference time without changing anything permanently.

Think of prompting as the instructions you give a new employee. Fine-tuning is more like retraining them entirely — different person, not different instructions.
